Inspiration¶

I have saw that the classmate's willingness to recite English vocabularies is very low. They admitted that "if memorising vocabularies is a game, then they must fight for the first place." This statement prompted me to create a game that helps memorising them. To my surprise, the project received enthusiastic response from my classmates. There were unexpectedly many people who were willing to participate. Luckily, the time also coincided with the period of self-learning courses. Therefore, the production of ⟪Hsueh Tse Tan⟫ officially kicked off.
