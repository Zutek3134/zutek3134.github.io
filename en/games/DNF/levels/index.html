Fall 1　

  • Icon

  • Gamerule

    Use the scroll bar to control the player in an ever-elevating environment.

    Reach the highest score in the limited time.

  • Mechanics

    • Safe

      Brown platform, Bouncing Pad, White disappearing platform.

    • Death

      Red platform, Top and Bottom of screen.

    • Powerup

      Slow down, Score up. Spawns randomly.

  • Achievements

    5 Normal; 1 Hidden.

Fall 2　

  • Icon

  • Gamerule

    On desktop, use W A S D or arrow keys to control player movement, and Space to shoot. For mobile users, tap the left screen to use joystick and the right screen to shoot. (Lefty reverses.)

    You gain scores when you move to a new hexa-pillar or knock down a small enemy. Shooting costs 1 point.

    Reach the highest score in the limited time.

  • Mechanics

    • Hexa-pillar

      Ground. Safe. Only exists temporarily. Flee to another one before it disappears.

    • Small Enemy

      Spawns randomly and tries to push the players off the hexa-pillar.

  • Achievements

    2 Normal; 2 Hidden.

Preference Range Default Function
Lefty on, off off Reverse controls
Opacity 0 ~ 100% 100% 0% to hide joystick
Countdown Mode progress bar, number progress bar As name suggests

Fall 3　

  • Icon

  • Gamerule

    Fly over a certain distance to earn Distance Bonus. Land near an edge to get Edge Bonus.

    • Vertical Scrollbar

      Control jump power.

    • Horizontal Scrollbar

      Control jump angle.

    • Undo Powerup

      Back to previous launch position.

    • Send Down Powerup

      Sends the player down immediately.

  • Mechanics

    • Normal Building

      Has friction. Safe.

    • Bouncy Building

      Low friction. Player bounces.

    • Magnetic Building

      Attracts or pushes the player away if in range.

    • Icy Building

      Zero friction. Player glides.

  • Achievements

    4 Normal; 2 Hidden.
