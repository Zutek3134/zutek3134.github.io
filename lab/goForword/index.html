Go Forword

Transform the starting word into the target word using as few steps as possible. This is a daily puzzle game of adding, removing, and changing letters.

I. How It Works

The dots are chances. The starting word is “no,” and the target word is “ye.”

The Master Par-ist is earnt if the puzzle is solved within the minimum possible number of steps.

Modify the word 1 letter at a time using 3 methods:

1

5

Deleting letters.

With these 3 simple methods, the puzzle is solved!

But, only 1 change per step.

And only valid words allowed. (Don’t worry, we have a massive dictionary of 370 K+ words.)

Undo the last step without penalty if needed.

The Lexical Purist is earnt if only common words are used.

There is no official solution. So be creative! Seek alternate solutions after solving the puzzle. However, only the 1st attempt would be recorded.

II. Schedule & Difficulty

A new puzzle drops daily at 00:00 local time. Difficulty scales from Easy up to Scholar throughout the week.

During weekends, an additional puzzle is available. Clear the easy one first to unlock the harder challenge.

III. The Archive

Access past puzzles back in time matching your current active daily streak count. Requires signing in.

IV. Personal

Sign in with a Discord account to sync stats, track daily streaks, and secure your cloud backup.

Note: Only your first puzzle attempt each day is officially recorded to the cloud.

How to Play

Can you transform the word?

A daily word chain transformation challenge. Step by step, shift one letter at a time to reach the destination target.

1

Use Real Words

Every word you enter must be a valid word, either a common or an uncommon word.

2

One Step Shift

Change exactly one letter at a time. You can add, substitute, or delete letters.

3

Check The Boundaries

You will be provided with a starting word and a target word. Work your way towards the end!
