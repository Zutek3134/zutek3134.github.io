Go Forword

New Game On The Block! As much as I hope that you don’t have to, please expect bugs. I am glad to test launch this game after 24 hours of development, and I am grateful for your anticipation. If you managed to find any issue, please contact me. Thanks!

Transform the starting word into the target word using as few steps as possible. This is a daily puzzle game of adding, removing, and changing letters.

You can access this page again by clicking on the question mark “?” after closing.

I. How It Works

Every day, 00:00 at your time zone, you will be greeted with 2 words.

Target Word
ye
0
no

As demonstrated above, your target word is “ye,” and your starting word is “no.” Your goal is to modify the starting word, 1 letter at a time, until it becomes the target word.

Below the target word is a row of dots. Those are your chances. For this question, you have a maximum of 8 chances, and the recommended solution is within 5 steps. You can tell by the size and the shade of the dots.

(This is just an example. I made up the numbers. But in the game it is all carefully calculated, and they are different for each puzzle.)

You can earn a trophy emoji (🏆) if you solved the puzzle within the recommended number of steps.

You can substitute letters.

You can delete letters.

With these 3 simple methods, the puzzle is solved!

You can use them during any step, on any letter. The only constraint is you may only use it 1 at a time.

Out of kindness, you can undo your latest step, and no penalties would be given.

There is no official solution. So be creative! You can also seek alternate solutions after you’ve solved your puzzle. However, only your first attempt would be recorded.

II. Difficulty

The game provides 5 tiers of difficulties, Easy, Medium, Hard, Advanced, and Scholar. It progresses throughout the week. During the weekend, you can unlock a harder puzzle after clearing the easier one.

You can switch between the weekend puzzles by clicking on the label on the left of the question mark that opens this tab.

III. The Time Traveller

You can access previous puzzles (up to 20th May, 2026) after you solve the one for today.

This is a time-limited feature. Play it while you can!

IV. Word List

All target and starting words are grabbed from the Google 10,000 English (No Swears) list, organised by Josh Kaufman, and all solutions are ensured to be possible using just only this list. Are you able to solve with only (or mostly) everyday words? You’ll earn a busts_in_silhouette emoji (👥) if you do!

first20hours/google-10000-english

However, for those who’ve eaten the whole dictionary, an additional list of English words by dwyl is also added, which includes over 466 thousand English words. Have fun using the most random word ever to solve the game. A hat shows up when you use a word exclusive to this list. You’ll know it when you see it.

If you have any suggestions, you may contact me on Discord via my server. Have fun solving!
