# About
!!! warning "This page is incomplete."

Although ⟪ALJ⟫ is basically the same as the original ⟪DL⟫, there are some differences. Here are them:

## I. Collectibles
There are **10 blocks** and **0 ~ 3 spheres** in each level.

{==

Spheres are **not necessity of levels**. They are merely **easter eggs**.

Spheres are placed on abnormal paths in the level, and the player can choose whether to collect it or not. Whatever the choice is, it will not affect the final result.

==}

## II. Lights
There is a light per level; players can lit them up for each level. There are 3 kinds of lights, blue, orange, and purple.

=== "Blue"
	Pass at least 50% of the level.

=== "Orange"
	Pass the entire level, collect all cubes. All without using guidance lines.

=== "Purple"
	Orange's condition, plus collect all spheres, if any.

	!!! warning "Not all levels can be lit up with Purple Light."

## III. Guidance Lines
Show you the way.

{==

Guidance lines are available for every single level. However, to enable them, you must pass at least **the first checkpoint** of the level.

==}
