III. Collectibles　

There are 10 per level.

There are 0 to 3 per level. Not a necessity and merely a surprise. Placed on unexpected places in the level. Players can decide whether or not to collect it; it does not affect the final score.

IV. Light　

Players can light up lights for every level.

Type Condition
Blue Light Complete at least 50%.
Orange Light Pass the level, collect 10 cubes, no guidance lines enabled.
Purple Light Orange light's condition, collect all spheres if exist.
Purple light may be unlightable for some levels.

V. Locked Features　

Some special features are locked until the criteria is met.

Shows you da wae.

Available for all levels. To enable, at least pass the first checkpoint.

Birdwatching view. Everything is flat. Note that levels are not made specifically for 2D modes, so expect unexpected behaviours.

Available for all levels. To enable, at least have the orange light lit up.

VI. Account System　

The account is only used for saving game data, nothing else is recorded.

Inactive accounts will face deletion.

On the 1st day of every month, an email will be sent to users who “have not logged in for at least 28 days.” On the 28th day of the same month, those said accounts will be deleted forever.

When an account is logged in, local data and cloud data will start to sync. Normally, the larger values will replace the smaller ones. Only level progresses are synced to cloud.
