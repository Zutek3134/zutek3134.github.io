Information⚓︎

I. Rules⚓︎

Please do not send the same messages over and over, for example: sending the same text, emojis, image... etc.

Also, you shouldn't send random meaningless messages or randomly ping people; pinging then deleting is by no means disallowed.

This should be a friendly server, so don't be rude to others, don't cuss, don't post NSFW contents or sensitive topics, don't spread negative thoughts or hatred comments.

Keep in mind that advertising anything by any form is not allowed.

Please use common sense, what's not listed here doesn't necessary mean it's allowed.

II. Notes⚓︎

1. Where to Chat⚓︎

Conversations are forbidden in #👀┋share-anything. If you wish to start one, please open a thread!

Besides #💬┋lets-chat, you can also check out #fun for community posts. Use tags to filter through "Official" and "Community".

2. Regarding Threads⚓︎

Do not create spamming threads in #👀┋share-anything. You will get warned by @Xx#Gaurdian#xX. Please do it in #fun instead.

3. Channel Topics⚓︎

Channel topics can overwrite rules. Be sure to read them!

III. Punishments⚓︎

Imprison
Annoying others or breaking rules after being told not to.
Kick
Threatening us to do something (i.e. "Unmute me or I'll leave!").
Quarantine
Constantly breaking rules even after getting imprisoned.
Ban
Being toxic or unable to reason with. Leaking A Line's Journey packages.

Step one: read the channel topic of #Verify.
After verifying you can have access to a small part of the server.
Step two: choose a category in #Menu Role.
You can only do this after verifying! After choosing one or more categories you can have full access of the server and the category you've chosen.
