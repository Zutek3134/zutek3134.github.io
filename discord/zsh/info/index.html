Guidelines

01
Rules Only Apply In This Server

Please never report users from other servers or direct messages and request us to take action against them; the rules for this server apply exclusively to this server.

02
Chat Normally

Avoid sending the same messages repeatedly, like the same texts, emojis, images, and so on. Never send out random, meaningless messages. Do not ping anyone at random; pinging and then deleting, also known as “ghost pinging,” is strictly prohibited.

03
Act Civicly

This should be a friendly server, so prevent yourself from being disrespectful to others. For example, cursing, promoting vitriol, or harbouring unpleasant sentiments. Refrain from posting NSFW or sensitive content.

04
Use Common Sense

Bear mind as well that advertising in any manner is not permitted. What is not included here does not necessarily imply that it is authorised. Moderators hold final decision-making and interpretation rights.

Notes

05
What to Say

We welcome folks from all over the world. We communicate in both English, the world’s most spoken foreign language, and Mandarin, the world’s most spoken first language. Please utilise only either of these languages in main channels like #💬┋lets-chat whenever possible. You may freely express with your native language in your dedicated threads under #🌐┋lets-chat-in-your-lang. Thank you for understanding. If you want me to understand what you say, English and Mandarin (Traditional) are ideal.

06
Where to Chat

Conversations are not tolerated in #👀┋share-anything. If you would like to initiate one, please open a thread, or forward it to #💬┋lets-chat! In addition to #💬┋lets-chat, #fun features community posts. Use tags to sort among “Official” and “Community.” Do not have a strong command of English? No worries, find your mother language under #🌐┋lets-chat-in-your-lang and communicate freely with your hometown mates.

07
Regarding Threads

Do not create spamming threads in #👀┋share-anything; instead, do it in #fun. Be in awareness that #fun is not an area free of regulations.
